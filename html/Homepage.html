Hybrid Human-AI Decision Support for Enhanced Human Empowerment in Dynamic Situations

Our approach

At HumAIne, we aim to pioneer the development and integration of innovative AI
paradigms. Our goal is to make human-AI collaboration not only advanced but also
profoundly reliable, even in dynamic and unstructured environments across a variety
of industries.

Our efforts at HumAIne will yield results that span multiple layers of expertise,
including AI, Machine Learning (ML), Explainable AI (XAI), Collaborative AI,
Benchmarking, Semantics, Data, and Visualization towards providing hybrid decision-
making and support.

These layers will be integrated into the HumAIne Operating System, built on four
technological pillars: Active Learning (AL), Neuro-Symbolic Learning, Swarm Learning
(SL), and eXplainable AI (XAI). The HumAine OS enables AI solution creators to build
advanced Human-AI collaboration systems that outperform standalone AI and
individual human efforts. Importantly, these systems are designed to be user-
friendly, making advanced AI accessible to all.

We're paving the way for a future where AI enhances, rather than replaces, human
potential.
